PoCC – Proof of Condition Chain
Blockchain-based Fair Pay & Crop Accountability System for Agricultural Supply Chains

PoCC (Proof of Condition Chain) is a blockchain-powered agricultural accountability platform designed to protect farmers from unfair losses caused during post-harvest logistics.

In traditional supply chains, farmers often deliver good-quality produce, but if spoilage happens later—during transport, storage, or before retail delivery—intermediaries may falsely blame the farmer for “poor quality at origin.” This results in unfair payment deductions and loss of trust.

PoCC solves this by creating a transparent, tamper-resistant chain of custody for produce movement from Farmer → Transporter → Warehouse → Retailer. At every handoff, the system records condition evidence such as produce quality status, timestamps, and transfer stage. Using this proof trail, the platform identifies where spoilage occurred and determines who is responsible to pay fair compensation (“fair pay”).

Key Features
🌾 Blockchain-backed custody tracking for every stage of produce transfer
📸 Condition logging at each handoff to verify quality before and after transport
⚖️ Fair Pay Engine that identifies the responsible stakeholder when spoilage occurs
🔗 Tamper-resistant proof records to prevent false blame on farmers
🚚 Stage-based accountability logic across Farmer, Transporter, Warehouse, and Retailer
📊 Transparent chain-of-condition visibility for all participants
How it Works
If spoilage occurs before transfer from Farmer to Transporter, the Farmer is responsible.
If spoilage occurs during transport before delivery to Warehouse, the Transporter is responsible.
If spoilage occurs during storage / before transfer from Warehouse to Retailer, the Warehouse is responsible.

This ensures that payment liability is based on actual custody, not assumptions or manipulation.

Why PoCC Matters

PoCC promotes:

Fairness for farmers
Trust in agri-supply chains
Transparent accountability
Reduced disputes between stakeholders
A practical real-world use of blockchain for social impact

PoCC is built as a hackathon prototype to demonstrate how blockchain and condition verification can create a more just and transparent agricultural ecosystem.
